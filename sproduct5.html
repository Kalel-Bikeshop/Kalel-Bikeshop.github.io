Product Details

This fork is designed for a 27.5-inch wheel size as well. "AIR FORK" indicates that it features an air spring, a common feature in suspension forks. Air forks allow for adjustments to the spring rate by varying the air pressure, providing a customizable level of stiffness. "STRAIGHT" suggests that the fork has a straight steerer tube, as opposed to a tapered one. "QR" stands for quick release, which is a type of axle system that allows for easy removal of the wheel.
